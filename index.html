The Iron Yard

I wrote about my experiences while going through the Iron Yard program on Medium. Please check that out Here.
The Iron Yard is an in-person immersive web development training program designed to give professionals the knowledge necessary to enter the tech field as a junior developer. For the duration of the 3+ month program, I dedicated from 10-12 hours per day taking classes, studying, and interacting with the tech community through meetups. It was the most difficult thing I've done in my life so far...and also the greatest. The program helped me learn how to code, problem solve, and gave me the tools to continue learning and perfecting my skills.
